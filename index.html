No backend, no accounts, no tracking. Your transaction history and FIFO gains are computed and cached entirely in this browser. To do that the app talks directly to the public Nimiq network and a public price API (which only ever sees dates, never your addresses) — there's no server of ours in between. It's fully open-source, so you can verify every line.

Not tax advice. This tool is for informational purposes only and is not tax, accounting, legal, or financial advice. Figures are best-effort and may be incomplete: NIM/USD prices come from CoinGecko (via DefiLlama) and history from the Nimiq Albatross network. Staking and unstaking are treated as tax-neutral transfers, and staking rewards — validator/block rewards (via the protocol coinbase), restaked rewards, and payouts from any pool addresses you declare — are counted as income at the day's price. FIFO gains assume each disposal's cost basis comes from your prior looked-up acquisitions — NIM acquired before your earliest looked-up transaction or off-chain is treated as zero cost basis, which overstates gains. Always confirm with a qualified tax professional before filing.
